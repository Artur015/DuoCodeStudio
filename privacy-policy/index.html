Privacy Policy

How DuoCode Studio handles contact details, cookies, and analytics.

Last updated on May 27, 2026. This page explains what data may be processed when you use duocodestudio.com or contact DuoCode Studio.

What data we may collect

We only collect data that is relevant to running this website and responding to enquiries.

  • Contact details and project information that you send by email or Instagram.
  • Basic server log data collected by the hosting provider, such as IP address, browser, and visit time.
  • Website usage data through Google Analytics, but only after you accept analytics cookies.

Why we use the data

We use personal data only for clear business and website-related purposes.

  • To reply to enquiries and discuss website work with potential clients.
  • To prepare proposals, deliver services, and continue communication during a project.
  • To understand which pages are useful and improve the clarity of the website.

The main legal bases are pre-contract communication, legitimate interest in running the site, and consent for analytics cookies.

Cookies and Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics only after you actively accept analytics cookies. If you do not accept, analytics remains off.

You can change your choice at any time through the Cookie settings button in the footer. We do not use advertising or personalization cookies on this site.

Third parties

Some data may be processed by service providers that help run the website and communication.

  • The hosting provider that serves the website and stores technical logs.
  • Google Analytics, if you consent to analytics cookies.
  • Your email provider or Instagram, if you choose to contact us there.

How long data is kept

Enquiry emails and project messages are kept only as long as needed for communication, project follow-up, and basic legal or accounting obligations.

Analytics data is handled according to Google Analytics retention settings and only after user consent.

Your rights

You can ask for access to your personal data, correction of incorrect data, deletion where applicable, or withdraw your analytics consent.

If you want to make such a request, contact DuoCode Studio by email and we will review it as soon as reasonably possible.

Contact about privacy

If you have any questions about privacy, cookies, or the way your data is handled on this website, write to contact@duocodestudio.com.
